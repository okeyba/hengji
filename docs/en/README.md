Organized by feature. Regular users only need the "Getting started" section; read "Advanced" once you've enabled merchant pro features.

## Getting started (regular users / solo merchants)

- [Books & separation](books.md) — personal / business / investment books, and how to keep business and personal money apart.
- [Accounts & global sharing](accounts.md) — how to create accounts and share a real wallet across books.
- [Logging entries & transactions](recording.md) — record expense / income / transfer, view and manage transactions.
- [Overview · global overview · budgets](overview.md) — see a single book and your whole net worth, set monthly budgets.
- [Simple vs pro mode](modes.md) — minimal mode is the default; when to enable "merchant pro features".

## Advanced (merchants / small business — requires "merchant pro features")

- [Products & inventory](inventory.md) — product master data, moving weighted-average cost, stock-in, stocktake.
- [Orders · customers · A/R](orders.md) — create orders, recognize revenue on completion, credit sales and installment collection, aging & due dates.
- [Purchases · suppliers · A/P](purchases.md) — purchase orders, supplier profiles, payables and repayment.
- [Extra fees & formula engine](fees.md) — reusable tiered fees like commission / shipping, applied per order line.
- [Monthly reconciliation](reconciliation.md) — line-by-line clearing, gain/loss adjustment, inline entry.
- [Multi-currency](multi-currency.md) — self-managed currencies, FX rates, display currency, currency-exchange entries.
